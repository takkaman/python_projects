About this pod
The cars are located on Level 1, the cars are parked at the top of the entry ramp around the corner from the lift in dedicated bays marked with Win signs.
This car park is accessible 24 hours a day 7 days a week.
The carpark pass-card is attached to the car with a curly cord. Please do not remove the pass-card from the vehicle.
The parking pass is to be applied against the proximity sensor at the boom gate. DO NOT put the parking pass in the parking ticket slot at the boom gate.
Please use the parking pass to exit and enter the car park – DO NOT take a parking ticket from the ticket machine at the car park entrance. Doing so will make it impossible for the next member to leave the car park using the parking pass.
